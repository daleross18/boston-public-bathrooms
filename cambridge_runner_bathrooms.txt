===============================================================================
TIPS FOR RUNNERS
===============================================================================

- In WINTER, your best bets are: hospitals (#35-43), libraries (#32-33, #91-92),
  university buildings (#24, #27-29), City Hall (#30), T stations (#44-45),
  fire stations (#46), Portland Loos (#23, #26, #47), Brookline Public Safety
  Building (#94 - 24 hours), Fresh Pond Ranger Station (#15), and Brookline
  High School (#95).

- In SUMMER, nearly everything is open. The Charles River path has porta-
  potties roughly every mile on both sides.

- The Portland Loos at Harvard Sq and Cambridge Common are 24/7/365 and
  your most reliable emergency option.

- Hospital emergency room lobbies (#35 Cambridge Hospital) are open 24
  hours and nobody will question you using the restroom.

- For the interactive map with real-time info: bathroomaccess.com
