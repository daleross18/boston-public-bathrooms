## Features

- **103 locations** across Cambridge, Boston, Brookline, Watertown, and Brighton
- **Interactive map** with Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap
- **Filter by type**: Parks, libraries, hospitals, recreation centers, transit, etc.
- **Filter by season**: Year-round vs seasonal restrooms
- **24-hour locations** highlighted for emergency access
- **Detailed info**: Hours, addresses, and accessibility notes for each location
- **Color-coded markers** for different facility types (colorblind-friendly palette)

## Location Categories

- **Public Buildings**: Libraries, City Halls, Fire Stations
- **Hospitals & Health Centers**: 24-hour access options available
- **Parks & Public Spaces**: Charles River path facilities, playgrounds
- **Recreation Centers**: YMCAs, pools, gyms
- **Universities**: Harvard, MIT campus buildings with public restrooms
- **MBTA Stations**: Red Line stations with restroom access
- **Attractions**: Museums, markets, hotels with accessible restrooms

## Geographic Coverage

- **Cambridge**: Central Square, Harvard Square, MIT area, Fresh Pond, Charles River path
- **Boston**: Downtown, Waterfront, Back Bay, Charlestown, North End, South Boston, Dorchester
- **Brookline**: Brookline Village, Coolidge Corner, Parks
- **Watertown**: Main Street, Parks, Libraries
- **Brighton**: Libraries, Recreation facilities

## Quick Tips for Runners

### Winter (November-March)
Best year-round options:
- Hospitals (#35-43) - many with 24-hour access
- Public libraries
- University buildings
- City Hall buildings
- **Portland Loos** (24/7): Harvard Square (#23), Cambridge Common (#26), Central Square (#47)

### Summer (June-August)
- Charles River path has porta-potties roughly every mile on both sides
- Park facilities open seasonally
- Many more options available

### Emergency Access
- **CHA Cambridge Hospital** (#35) - ER open 24 hours, reception 6am-8pm
- **Mount Auburn Hospital** (#40) - Lobby open 24 hours
- **Brookline Police/Public Safety** (#94) - 24-hour accessible restrooms
- **Portland Loos** - 24/7/365

## Map Features

### Filtering
- Click category names in legend to show/hide location types
- Toggle "Year-round" and "Seasonal" checkboxes to filter by availability
- Dimmed markers indicate filtered-out locations

### Location Details
- Click any marker to view:
  - Full location name and address
  - GPS coordinates
  - Operating hours
  - Season (year-round or seasonal)
  - 24-hour indicator (if applicable)

### Color Scheme
- **Yellow** - Public Buildings
- **Blue** - Recreation Centers
- **Orange** - Parks & Public Spaces
- **Teal** - Sports Fields
- **Mauve** - University Buildings
- **Dark Blue** - Attractions
- **Burnt Orange** - Hospitals
- **Black** - MBTA Stations
- **Gray** - Other (hotels, retail)
